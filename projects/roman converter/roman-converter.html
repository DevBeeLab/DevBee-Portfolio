DevBee Roman Converter

A utility for converting between Arabic numbers and Roman numerals.

Converter

How to use:

  • Auto-detect mode automatically determines if you enter a number or Roman numeral
  • Manual mode lets you explicitly choose which conversion direction to use
  • Valid Roman numerals: I (1), V (5), X (10), L (50), C (100), D (500), M (1000)
  • Numbers must be between 1 and 3999
  • Your last 10 conversions are saved locally
  • Click on any history item to load it back

Roman numeral:

Quick Reference:
